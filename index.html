The Opportunity

Wound care has stayed
passive for decades

HealCue develops two patent-pending platforms — Smart Sutures and Smart Patches — that respond to the wound and deliver therapy on their own. No power, no electronics, no clinician intervention required at the point of care.

Founded and entirely bootstrapped by Dr. Mohammed N. Quadri, MD, MBA — a practicing physician and named inventor on both patents — this SPV is the company's first external capital. The founder has taken no salary and personally funded formation, IP, and active laboratory work.

The market opportunity spans $25B+ across surgical sutures, advanced wound care, and defense. Comparable exits — Acera Surgical (acquired by Solventum for up to $850M in 2025) and Embody (acquired by Zimmer Biomet for up to $275M in 2023) — validate both the technology category and the acquirer universe.

HealCue Mission
The Platforms

Two platforms,
one responsive-material approach

Power-Free
Smart Sutures™
A biodegradable composite suture engineered for pH-responsive drug delivery at the wound site. No electronics, no power source — built for any setting from the OR to the field.
  • Silk fibroin composite with pH-triggered release
  • Fully power-free, no electronics on the suture
  • Phase 1 bench work underway; silk fibroin extraction in progress
Chemistry-First + Optional Sensing
Smart Patches™
A multilayer nanofibrous scaffold with autonomous antimicrobial release matched to wound type. Chemistry-first with passive failsafe, plus an optional connected sensing layer.
  • Antimicrobial release matched by wound type
  • Material optimization and SEM morphology complete; FTIR active
  • Optional connected sensing layer for clinical monitoring
How It Works
1
Sense
The material responds to pH shifts and biochemical signals in the wound environment.
2

Therapy is delivered precisely where and when needed — no power, no external input.
3

Reduces infection risk and supports healing across the full course of recovery.
